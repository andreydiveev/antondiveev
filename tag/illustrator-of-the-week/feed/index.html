Nga Le Pham is a first year BA Fashion Design & Technology: Womenswear student at LCF. Using mixed media, her illustrations are beautifully thoughtful and convey a level of pensiveness. It sometimes seems as if the women portrayed in her pieces have developed thoughts of their own in all their stylised apparel. The use of black ink on white background makes for the greatest possible contrast and gives off a duality — for example, between good and evil, yin and yang or day and night. To see more of Nga’s work, go to her Tumblr.
