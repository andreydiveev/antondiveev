]]> Floss Given is back, with her saccharine, confrontational, feminist zine Don’t Call Me Baby.

Floss describes her zine as a ‘bible of fucking the male gaze’, and has spent the last 6 months creating, making, protesting, illustrating, shooting and writing, addressing the issues of sexual assault and harassment. The zine aims to challenge patriarchal opinions and empower women.

The zine features all of her own styled photo-shoots, illustrations and articles, and includes features from Xian Bass, and photography by Hannah Clark & Megan Lavis. Covergirl: Femi Hurley-Scott.
