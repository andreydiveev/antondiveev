]]> P&P Photographer and BA Fashion Photography graduate Orsolya Luca needs you!

She has 3 days left on her Kickstarter page set up to fund her limited edition book Dystopia.

Dystopia is an art based photographic exploration where she examines the social, cultural and political differences between her home town Budapest and her adopted city of London. Shot using an iPhone, she creates connections between different scenarios and whilst experimenting with identity, race and religion.

To find out more about the project and to back Dystopia visit Orsolya’s Kickstarter page.

We gave ourselves over to momentary happiness, with the wind over our heads and the sun throwing its dashing rays upon us. We were free. We were young. We still didn’t forget. The sky and its endless possibilities.  We sheltered our bodies and thoughts on rooftops, allowing ourselves to dream of a better future, of a better planet, of a better life. But we knew that what we had right then was ours. The moment was ours and so was the sky. That’s when we realized the sky was no longer a limit.
